Containers are nestable.

This means you can nest a container, inside a container, inside a container, inside a container, as much as you like.

<div class="container">
 <div class="container">
  <div class="container"> </div>
 </div>
</div>

On the left is a container
inside a three columns blue
inside the main container.

<div class="container">
 <div class="three columns blue">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="one column yellow"></div>
    <div class="one column red"></div>
    <div class="one column yellow"></div>
    <div class="one column red"></div>
    <div class="one column yellow"></div>
    <div class="one column red"></div>
  </div>
 </div>
  ...
</div>
